About Vision

Vision was founded in September 2010. The founder, Mr. Danny Wu, has more than 15years of experiences in domestic and foreign industries. Vision focus on the core of enterprise problems, provides high-quality as well as full-service in order to assist companies with improving their managerial capacity and competitiveness.


Our team is able to communicate with customers in both Chinese and English directly. Our services not only include in business registration, financial audit, bookkeeping, tax planning, but also in business mergers, business negotiations, crisis management, and strategic planning, investment assessment, corporate restructuring, corporate governance, on-line-business, and so on.


Because of the cross-strait business boom and Taiwan government’s New Southbound Policy, Vision has endeavored to help our customers redefine their business core values, analyze the business strategies in order to face the unpredictable business environment. Our goal is to inspire our clients to see the future and to create miracle.
